About RET in Panama

Since 2009, RET International has been a vital force in Panama, dedicated to bridging the gaps for vulnerable populations. We provide a protective environment for refugees, asylum seekers, and local youth, ensuring they have the tools to build a dignified future.

Our operations focus on three core pillars: Education as a tool for integration, Protection against discrimination, and Self-Reliance through vocational training and legal guidance.

About Puente: Your Education Specialist

Puente is a dedicated digital mentor focused exclusively on educational integration. Our goal is to ensure that every refugee has a clear path to learning in Panama.

"Focusing on what matters: Schooling, University, and Language. Puente removes the information barriers between you and your classroom."

Puente provides expert guidance on Panama's public school system, documentation for degree recognition, and local language training. By specializing only in education, Puente ensures higher accuracy and faster support for students and parents.
